Thank you for your interest in joining Net Impact Berkeley. Recruiting takes place at the beginning of every semester, and is open to students of all majors, years and backgrounds. During the beginning of the semester, you can find us at Calapalooza, on Sproul Plaza, or at our two infosessions. Please come visit us at any of these to learn more about our organization and consultants. We highly recommend candidates to attend one of two info sessions to learn more about NIB and ask any questions. For any other questions related to recruitment, please contact nibundergrad@gmail.com.
