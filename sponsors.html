Our Sponsors

GOAL

Through sponsorships, we hope to increase member exposure to professional opportunities, provide an avenue through which to host quality external events on campus, and build partnerships to support our consulting practice and professional development services to members.

SERVICES

Net Impact Berkeley offers a number of services to its sponsors including marketing services to improve the sponsor's brand awareness among the student body, room reservations for on campus events, a professional relationship with our members, and campus connections to other professional organizations and socially responsible groups.

NEXT STEPS

For more information about sponsoring Net Impact Berkeley, please refer to our sponsorship packet. For any additional questions and sponsorship inquiries, please contact us at nibundergrad@gmail.com.
